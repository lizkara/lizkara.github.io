    About Me

    Hi there! Nice to meet you.

    I am an Interactive Designer from the greater Chicago Area with a passion for creating beautiful, modern, clean and functional designs. I specialize in a wide range of interface design including: websites, smartphone applications, tablets, emails, and kiosks. Throughout my years as a designer I have obtained skills that have allowed me to successfully work in an in-group atmosphere.

    I am reliable, proficient, meticulously detailed, very focused as a perfectionist, which makes me the perfect resource for pixel-perfect designs and complex interfaces. I have also reached an expert proficiency in the Adobe Suite. In addition to my interactive design skills, I have acquired the skills of site mapping, wireframes, UX design, and user architecture.

    My time-management and organizational skills are excellent. I am ambitious and love taking on new challenges. I have a strong passion for design and I am constantly learning the latest trends in the industry and improving best practices in interface design.

    I love spending my time outdoors, particularly biking along the lakefront. Apart from design, I also enjoy listening to music. Ever since I’ve been introduced to Spotify my music playlist has tripled with new artists and bands. You can also find me hanging out with friends, dining at restaurants, cooking my favorite recipes, going to the beach, going to cubs and bears games, or simply catching up on Dexter and Homeland.
